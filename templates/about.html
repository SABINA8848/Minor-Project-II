About Arthemis

Arthemis is an apparel & clothing store situated at the capital of Nepal, Kathmandu. The company specializes in womenâ€™s fashion and holds various products including clothing, accessories, shoes, and swimsuits.
Arthemis was founded in the year 2019 with the purpose to provide quality products at a very reasonable and affordable price.
Initially Arthemis opened its first outlet in New Road (Heart of Kathmandu). On the journey the purpose became the culture for Arthemis. This culture has enabled Arthemis to become one of the most recognized fashion stores in the country.
To make shopping easily accessible, Arthemis launched its four outlets inside Kathmandu valley and seven stores outside the Kathmandu valley. Currently, Arthemis also holds an international outlet in Thailand.
